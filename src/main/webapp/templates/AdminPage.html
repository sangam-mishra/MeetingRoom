Manage Meeting Rooms with Ease

As an administrator, you have full control over the meeting rooms within HSBC Technology. This platform allows you to efficiently manage the availability, configuration, and details of each meeting room.

What you can do:

  • Create New Rooms: Add new meeting rooms to the system with essential details like room name, capacity, location, and available amenities.
  • Edit Existing Rooms: Modify the details of existing meeting rooms to reflect changes in capacity, amenities, or other configurations.
  • Monitor Room Availability: Get an overview of the current status of all meeting rooms, including upcoming bookings and any maintenance schedules.
  • Ensure Efficient Utilization: Make sure all rooms are properly utilized by adjusting settings, removing unused rooms, and optimizing space allocation.
