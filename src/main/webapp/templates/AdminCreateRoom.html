Create a New Meeting Room with Ease!

As an administrator, you have the ability to create and manage meeting rooms tailored to your organization's needs. By providing key details and selecting the right amenities, you can ensure that every meeting has the perfect environment.

Here's how to fill out the form:

  • Room Name: Enter a unique name for the meeting room. This helps everyone easily identify and select the room during booking.
  • Location: Specify the room's location within the building. Include the floor number or any specific details to help users find the room effortlessly.
  • Capacity: Indicate the maximum number of people the room can accommodate. This ensures that the room is booked for meetings of appropriate size.
  • Amenities: Check the amenities available in the room and assign credits for each. Amenities like a whiteboard, projector, or coffee machine can enhance the meeting experience. Specify the credit cost for each amenity to manage resource allocation effectively.
  • Create Room: Once all fields are filled out, click the "Create Room" button to finalize and save the room details. Make sure all information is correct before submitting.

This form allows you to set up rooms that meet your team's specific needs, ensuring that every meeting is productive and well-equipped. Take a moment to review the details before creating the room.
